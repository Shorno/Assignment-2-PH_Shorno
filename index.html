BMI Calculator

Quickly assess your weight status with our BMI calculator. Simply enter your height and weight to get an estimate of your body mass index. While BMI isn't a perfect measure, it provides a general indicator of whether you're underweight, healthy weight, overweight, or obese. Use this tool as a starting point for discussing your health goals with a healthcare professional.
